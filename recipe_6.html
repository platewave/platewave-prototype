Recipe
Mushroom Risotto
Earthy, creamy, and the ultimate Sunday comfort food. The stirring is meditative — that's part of the point.
10 minPrep
30 minCook
2Serves
Ingredients
Arborio rice1 cup
Mixed mushrooms, sliced250g
Vegetable stock, warm4 cups
Dry white wine½ cup
Shallot, finely diced1
Garlic cloves, minced2 cloves
Parmesan, grated½ cup
Butter + olive oil2 tbsp each
Steps
01
NowSauté mushrooms in 1 tbsp butter over high heat until golden, about 5 minutes. Season with salt. Set aside.
02
In the same pan, heat olive oil over medium. Add shallot and garlic, cook 2 minutes. Add rice and stir to coat, 1 minute.
03
Add wine and stir until absorbed. Begin adding warm stock one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and letting each ladle absorb before adding the next. This takes about 20 minutes.
04
When rice is al dente and creamy, stir in remaining butter and parmesan. Fold in mushrooms.
05
Season generously, add a splash of stock if too thick. Serve immediately in warm bowls with extra parmesan.
